Slash pages

Slash pages are pages in a website that can be found in well-known locations. The name refers to how the pages can be found right at the root of a site, so they start with a slash, e.g. /about. A list of all the slash pages with further information can be found at slashpages.net. Here follows a list of the slash pages on this website.

  • /about
  • /blank
  • /colophon
  • /quotes
  • /slashes
