About me

Currently, I'm pursuing my bachelor's degree in computer science. That takes most of my time. What remains I spend bringing down my to-read pile, and fiddling with personal coding projects.

The coding projects I'm proudest of are pinned on my GitHub profile. Some of them are university group projects. I dedicated a lot of time on each of them, to make them beautiful and usable, to make them just right. I find satisfaction in that, coding becomes like woodcraft.

I've also a latent interest in Rust. I love when my code is compiled to assembly and gobbled up directly by the CPU, no interpretation, no bytecode virtual machines, but bare, overheated metal.

As for my to-read pile, I track my progress on StoryGraph. I read anything, even though I love a good fantasy or a clever science fiction premise to a story. I'm careful to not reduce reading to a chore, so I don't do challenges, and I'm in fact a rather slow reader.
